    About Your Tabagotchi

    Your Tabagotchi is a companion that helps you control your habit of opening tabs without noticing how much resources its taking. The Tabagotchi is going to remind you with the fun activites of the popular toy Tamagotchi.

    • Mood: Reflects the state of your Tabagotchi based on your activity, it is affected by the amount of tabs open and open for how long.

    • Actions: Feed or play with your Tabagotchi to keep it happy, sleeping disables all functions for 10 mins.

    • Exclusions: Manage websites where you want the tabagotchi not to get grumpy if open for too long. Example: Youtube.
